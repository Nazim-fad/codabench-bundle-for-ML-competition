## Competition Timeline

This competition operates under a single, continuous evaluation phase. Participants have ongoing access to the training data and can submit their models at any time for immediate scoring against the **public test set**.

### 📅 Important Dates

**Phase 1: Currently Open**
* Training data, baseline models, and ingestion environments are live.
* Submissions are actively scored against the public test set.

**Final Evaluation & Deadline: August 31, 2026 at 02:00 AM CEST (UTC+2)**
* **Portal Closes:** The submission portal closes strictly at 02:00 AM CEST. No late submissions will be accepted. 
* **Final Ranking:** After the deadline, your selected models will be evaluated against the entirely hidden **private test set** to determine the final leaderboard rankings.
